Savannah Cats

About Savannah

Savannah is a domestic cat hybrid, cross bred between a african serval and a domestic cat. These cats are idetified as a new registered breed in 2001 by the International Cat Association and are recognized as a champion breed in 2012 by the International Cat Association. Depending on the generation of cross from their wild ancestory; cats are characterized by f1 filial for first generation, f2 for second generation and f3 for third generation with any farther distant generation classified as SBG for Stud Book Tradition. Male hybrids are generally sterile in until the f5 generation, while females f1 females are fertile.
